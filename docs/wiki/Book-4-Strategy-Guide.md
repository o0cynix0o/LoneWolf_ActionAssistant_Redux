# Book 4 Strategy Guide - *The Chasm of Doom*

A heads-up before we begin: this guide talks openly about routes, items, danger spots, and replay targets for Book 4.

Book 4 is an investigation that slowly becomes a crisis. It starts with missing cavalry and road trouble, then pulls you toward mines, cultists, and one very bad dagger with a long shadow.

## First-Clear Mindset

Do not rush Book 4 like a straight dungeon crawl. You are following clues, managing carried gear, and trying to reach the real threat with enough Endurance left to finish the job.

Good first-run habits:

- keep Endurance-restoring items if you can
- respect equipment choices at the start
- carry light enough that new mine gear is not impossible to manage
- do not assume every key is safe just because it is shiny
- use the combat presets for Barraka and other audited fights

## Campaign Handoff

Continuing from Book 3 gives you another Kai Discipline, Book 4 gear choices, starting Gold, and any legal carry-forward items. The app also supports fresh Book 4 creation for standalone play.

The Padded Leather Waistcoat and Chainmail Waistcoat stacking rule is already handled. Duplicate chainmail still does not stack.

## Items Worth Respecting

**Dagger of Vashna** is the major Book 4 reward. The app adds it on completion and marks it as a series item in inventory.

**Onyx Medallion** is a route item worth keeping once you find it.

**Flask of Holy Water** is useful enough to earn its own achievement.

**Iron Key** is dangerous. Taking it can be a replay target, but it is not a first-clear recommendation.

**Mine treasure** can include healing and combat-helping items. If you are achievement hunting, section 302 is worth revisiting.

## Paths Worth Knowing

### The Ruanon Road

The early road sections are there to drain confidence and resources. If the app offers gear or route helpers, use them. Book 4 likes to make small inventory choices matter later.

### The Maaken Mines

The mines are where utility gear starts to compete with survival gear. Pickaxes, shovels, ropes, torches, and keys are all practical, but Backpack space is still real.

### Barraka

Barraka is the fight everyone remembers. If the app starts a Barraka preset, let it handle the special combat details. This is not the place to eyeball the math and hope.

## Combat Tips

Book 4 has more combat texture than the earlier books: enemy immunities, timing rules, underwater pressure, and one-round comparison routing. The app now has presets for the scanned fights, and the full audit watches for route targets that do not match the book text.

## Achievement Cleanup

Book 4 achievements now cover:

- complete the book
- carry forward the Dagger of Vashna
- take the Onyx Medallion
- recover the mine treasure cache
- win a Barraka combat
- take a Flask of Holy Water
- take the dangerous Iron Key
- visit 90 or more unique Book 4 sections

The safest first clear is not the same thing as the fullest achievement run. That is a feature, not a punishment.
