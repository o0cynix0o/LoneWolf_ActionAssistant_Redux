# Stats and Achievements

The app tracks book progress and achievements as part of the save.

## Stats

Book 1 progress can include:

- current section
- section history
- unique sections visited
- current Combat Skill and Endurance
- Gold Crowns
- carried Weapons, Backpack Items, and Special Items
- combat history
- death and recovery history
- completed books

## Achievement Types

Book 1 achievements can be tied to:

- book completion
- reaching Holmgard
- the clean messenger route
- first combat victory
- the Gourgaz fight
- item discoveries such as the Prince's Sword and Crystal Star Pendant
- paid route choices
- dangerous Vordak Gem outcomes
- capture escape and failure branches
- gear loss
- broad exploration

## Unlock Alerts

When an achievement unlocks, the app shows a browser notification-style alert so you notice it during play.

Unlocked achievements remain visible in the Achievements tab.

## Backfill

Achievements sync automatically from save history when the app loads or serves state.

That means if the save already contains durable evidence that you reached a section, completed a book, won a fight, picked up an item, or triggered a story event, the app can unlock the achievement without a separate manual rebuild button.

## Replay Targets

Some achievements are naturally replay targets.

For example, if a route was never taken or a choice was never made, that achievement should stay locked until a later run.

For cleanup advice, see the [100 Percent Achievement Guide](Achievement-100-Percent-Guide).

## Repeat Book

Use **Repeat Book 1** when you want another run at routes or achievements. The app returns you to section 1, restores full Endurance and base Combat Skill, rebuilds your original Book 1 starting gear, and keeps your Kai Disciplines and Weaponskill.

Achievement history and combat history stay in the save, so a replay feels like a fresh run without erasing what you already proved.
