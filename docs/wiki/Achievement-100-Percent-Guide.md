# Achievement 100 Percent Guide

This page is for achievement cleanup after you already know Book 1.

If you are still on your first run, use the [Book 1 Strategy Guide](Book-1-Strategy-Guide) instead. A clean finish matters more than forcing every dangerous branch immediately.

## Clean First Clear

Start with the story clear. It should unlock the core completion achievements and give you a safe save history to build from.

Use the route in [Full Campaign Story Run](Full-Campaign-Story-Run) if you want a steady path to Holmgard.

## Replay Cleanup

After the first clear, come back for the dangerous and optional branches:

- Gourgaz and the Prince's Sword
- Vordak Gem outcomes
- capture escape and captive death
- paid ferry and caravan routes
- Crystal Star Pendant
- broad exploration for Long Road to Holmgard

These are better replay targets than first-run chores.

## Backfill

The app backfills achievements automatically from save history when it has reliable evidence. If a route or item never happened in the save, the achievement should stay locked until you actually do it.
