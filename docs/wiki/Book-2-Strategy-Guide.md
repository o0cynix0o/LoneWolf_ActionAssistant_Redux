# Book 2 Strategy Guide

This page is for **Fire on the Water**. It assumes you already know the Book 1 basics: protect Endurance, respect Meals, and do not treat every shiny side path as free.

## First-Clear Mindset

Book 2 asks you to do three big things:

- reach Durenor with the Seal of Hammerdal
- claim the Sommerswerd
- get back to Holmgard alive

For a first clear, play steady. Keep Gold Crowns available for travel and passes, keep at least one Meal when you can, and do not sell or discard the Seal unless you are deliberately exploring a bad idea.

## Campaign Handoff

If you are continuing from Book 1, the app carries your character forward and then applies the Book 2 start:

- choose one new Kai Discipline
- roll Book 2 starting Gold, capped at 50
- keep your carried Weapons, Backpack Items, and Special Items
- add the Seal of Hammerdal
- choose Royal Armoury gear

If Book 2 is your first book, use the fresh Book 2 character option. That creates a legal starting character without pretending you completed Book 1.

## Helpful Disciplines

These are especially comfortable in Book 2:

- **Sixth Sense**: frequent route warnings and Sommerswerd insight.
- **Tracking**: helps with road and city navigation.
- **Healing**: steady Endurance recovery still matters.
- **Hunting**: useful when the terrain allows it, though Book 2 has places where the text blocks Hunting.
- **Mindshield**: protects against Helghast Mindforce.
- **Animal Kinship**: opens some gentler creature routes.
- **Weaponskill**: very strong if your weapon match lines up with the Sommerswerd or Magic Spear.

No single set is mandatory, but a first run feels kinder with route-reading and Endurance protection.

## Inventory Tips

- Keep the **Seal of Hammerdal** safe. It is not flavor text.
- The **Sommerswerd** is a Special Item, but the app treats it as a combat weapon when you have it.
- The **Magic Spear** is also a weapon-like Special Item for the fights that care about it.
- Gold caps at 50. If you are already at the cap, extra Gold is simply lost.
- Backpack Items from Book 1 can matter later, so do not throw things away just to make the sheet look neat.

## Combat Notes

The app now has Book 2 combat presets for the checked fights. It handles the big special rules:

- Sommerswerd Combat Skill bonus
- Sommerswerd double damage against undead enemies
- Magic Spear weapon handling
- Shield Combat Skill bonus
- no-weapon penalties
- Helghast Mindforce effects
- arm-wrestling END restoration

When a fight has an audited preset, use the Combat panel rather than doing the math by hand.

## Manual Moments

Two gambling sections remain manual because the player chooses stakes or target numbers:

- Cartwheel
- Portholes

The app still tracks your Gold if you adjust it, but it does not guess your wager.

## Achievement Cleanup

Finish the book once before chasing every unlock. Good replay targets include:

- Magic Spear routes
- Green Sceptre disaster branches
- Port Bax pass outcomes
- forged access papers
- Helghast fights
- broad exploration for Across The Lastlands

If a choice looks obviously unwise, it probably belongs in a replay run. We say this with affection and a small amount of hard-earned concern.
