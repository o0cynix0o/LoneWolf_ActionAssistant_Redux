# Book 5 Fandom Polish Review

Scope: Shadow on the Sand polish pass using fan-wiki pages as secondary context.

Canonical rule source remains the local Project Aon Book 5 files. The fan wiki is used to prioritize guide polish and campaign-transition achievements.

## Sources Reviewed

- https://lonewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Shadow_on_the_Sand
- https://lonewolf.fandom.com/wiki/Shadow_on_the_Sand/Walkthrough

## Useful Signals

- Book 5 is the final Kai-series book and leads into the Magnakai ruleset.
- The book has a two-part structure, with Barrakeesh/palace escape followed by the wider Vassagonian route.
- Gear confiscation/recovery, Black Sash, Itikar escape, Jewelled Mace, and Book of the Magnakai are strong guide and achievement targets.
- Safekeeping matters more now because the campaign is approaching a rules-family transition.

## Decisions

- Add Book 5 achievements for completion, Book of the Magnakai, Kai Master, Black Sash, gear recovery, Jewelled Mace, Itikar victory, and broad exploration.
- Add a Book 5 strategy guide that warns players about the two-part structure and gear handling.
- Add inventory key-item notes for Black Sash, Jewelled Mace, and Book of the Magnakai.
