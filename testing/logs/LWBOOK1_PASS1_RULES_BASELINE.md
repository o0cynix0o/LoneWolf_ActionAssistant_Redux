## Character Creation

- Combat Skill is the first random digit plus 10.
- Endurance is the second random digit plus 20.
- A Book 1 character chooses exactly five Kai Disciplines.
- Weaponskill, if chosen, uses a random digit to pick the skilled weapon.
- Weaponskill gives +2 Combat Skill only when the matching weapon is carried and used.
- Mindblast gives +2 Combat Skill unless the enemy is stated to be immune.

## Starting Equipment

Base gear:

- Axe under Weapons.
- Backpack containing 1 Meal.
- Belt Pouch containing Gold Crowns equal to a random digit.
- Map of Sommerlund under Special Items.

Monastery find roll:

- 1: Sword.
- 2: Helmet, +2 Endurance.
- 3: two Meals.
- 4: Chainmail Waistcoat, +4 Endurance.
- 5: Mace.
- 6: Healing Potion.
- 7: Quarterstaff.
- 8: Spear.
- 9: 12 Gold Crowns.
- 0: Broadsword.

Capacity rules:

- Maximum 2 Weapons.
- Maximum 8 Backpack Items, including Meals.
- Gold Crowns cap at 50.

## Combat Baseline

- If Lone Wolf has no weapon, deduct 4 Combat Skill.
- Only one Weapon is used at a time.
- The Combat Results Table is still used for round resolution.
- Full section-specific combat exceptions remain unaudited in this pass.
