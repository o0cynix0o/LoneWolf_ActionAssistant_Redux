# Book 4 Fandom Polish Review

Scope: The Chasm of Doom polish pass using fan-wiki pages as secondary context.

Canonical rule source remains the local Project Aon Book 4 files. The fan wiki is used to prioritize player guidance, carry-forward warnings, and achievement candidates.

## Useful Signals

- The Dagger of Vashna is the key Book 4 carry-forward reward.
- The walkthrough highlights useful previous-book items such as Sommerswerd, Firesphere, Padded Leather Waistcoat, and Silver Helm.
- Mine treasure, Holy Water, Onyx Medallion, and Barraka are good replay/achievement anchors.
- Some loot is dangerous or marginal, which belongs in the guide rather than automatic restrictions.

## Decisions

- Add Book 4 achievements for completion, Dagger of Vashna, Onyx Medallion, mine treasure, Barraka victory, Holy Water, Iron Key danger, and broad exploration.
- Add a Book 4 strategy guide focused on investigation, gear pressure, and replay targets.
- Add inventory key-item notes for Onyx Medallion and Dagger of Vashna.
